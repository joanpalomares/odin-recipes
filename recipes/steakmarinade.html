Steak Marinade

Ingredients

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tablespoons dried basil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons garlic powder
  • 1 ½ tablespoons dried parsley flakes
  • 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried minced garlic (Optional)

Steps

  1. Place olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, basil, garlic powder, parsley, white pepper, and minced garlic in a blender; blend on high speed until thoroughly combined, about 30 seconds.
